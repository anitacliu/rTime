Physical Health

Working from home, especially if it's uncharted territory, is an extreme shift in our daily routines. There is no commuting, in-person interactions, and going to the gym. Unfortunately, being physically active often falls to the back of our minds when sitting all day working on projects.

However, it is still just as vital to our health to move around a bit, especially after staring at a screen to work for hours on end. rTime's Physical Health resources are here to help you to say healthy and in a good space at home!

Why Should You Exercise in COVID-19?

Exercising boosts our immune system to help fight off infections such as COVID-19. It can mitigate weight gain, which comes in handy at the sedentary level of activity that accompanies remote work. Exercise reduces stress and anxiety levels. Additionally, it can even get you a good night's sleep!

Tips on Taking Care of Your Physical Self

Even at home, you should set a reasonable daily routine for your general well-being. Include breaks and resting time throughout your day. Though it may be easy to eat junk food at home, it is important to eat healthy and regularly to maintain physical health. Get a full night's sleep and exercise. Be sure to move around while working and keep your blood flowing!

Yoga for COVID-19

Yoga offers many benefits that are specifically helpful in tough times like these. For one, practicing yoga may rid of bothersome bodily pains while at home. As well as increasing fitness, it also can improve your night of sleep as well as your focus during the day! Finally, yoga also reduces stress that may occur in your COVID-19 environment.
